Titanic dataset analysis:¶

I have choosed the titanic data set for analysis , Following are the question i would like to answer in provided dataset.

  1. Is there any relation between the sex of the passenger who is travelling and the chances of his or her survival(does any sympathy shown towards the particular sex).
  2. Does we have any relationship between the age of the person and chances of his/her survival(does any sympathy shows toward the childern and older people)
  3. Is there any relation between the class of passenger travelling and chances of his or her survival(does any preference given on the basis of class)

def barplot(dataval,variable,binvalues,binlabel,xlabel,ylabel,titleval,ydes,hueval):
    dataval.loc[:,xlabel] = pd.cut(dataval[variable], bins=binvalues, labels=binlabel)
    if hueval==False:  
        ax=sns.barplot(x=xlabel, y=ylabel, data=dataval,ci=None)
        ax.set(ylabel=ydes,title=titleval)
        tickval=ax.get_yticks()
        ax.set_yticklabels(['{:3.2f}%'.format(x*100) for x in tickval]) 
    else:
        ax=sns.barplot(x=xlabel, y=ylabel,hue=hueval, data=dataval,ci=None)
        ax.set(ylabel=ydes,title=titleval)
        tickval=ax.get_yticks()
        ax.set_yticklabels(['{:3.2f}%'.format(x*100) for x in tickval]) 
    plt.show()

def barplot_for_per(Dataset,fromsex,tosex):
    short=Dataset[Dataset['Sex']==tosex]
    short[short['Survived']==1]
    Dataset['Survived']=short['Survived']
    Dataset['Survived'].fillna(0,inplace=True)
    Dataset['Sex']=Dataset['Sex'].replace(fromsex,tosex)
    ax=sns.barplot(x="Sex", y="Survived", data=Dataset)
    ax.set(ylabel='Pecentage of survival',title='Survival of a particular sex')
    tickval=ax.get_yticks()
    ax.set_yticklabels(['{:3.2f}%'.format(x*100) for x in tickval])  
    plt.show()

def pointplot(dataset,x_val,y_val,y_label,title_val):
    ax=sns.pointplot(x=x_val, y=y_val, data=dataset);
    ax.set(ylabel=y_label,title=title_val)
    tickval=ax.get_yticks()
    ax.set_yticklabels(['{:3.2f}%'.format(x*100) for x in tickval])
    plt.grid()
    plt.show()

Question :

Does our analysis having any limitation or shortcoming.

Answer :

Yes, Since during the data cleaning phase we have stub age data for 167 passenger. the approach we used to fill this data is to compute the mean of age on the basis of Sex and Pclass.Since we have some artifical age data for 167 passenger , there quite a few chance that our analysis may not stand exactly true. but it seems that trends would be same as we have presented (since we have done our best to neturalise the real data effect by taking the mean of age on the basis of sex and travel class)

we have also put the artifical data for cabin(using random number) and embarkation(using bfill function) in our dataset. But that would not effect our analysis as we have not used these fields for our analysis.

Does the trends that we have seen in our analysis are significant?

Answer:

After answering all the questions we can have find out there some clear indications which suggest the correlation betwen the different varibales(like travelclass and chances of survival, preference give to particular sex while survival)

We can further check out whether these correlation are significant or not using statistics. Since we are provided with the sample dataset of traveller of titanic we can carry out the T-Test statistics , to check whether this co-relation has any significance or not.
